Rice Recipe

This is a simple recipe that you can make at home within 1 hour. Rice is an essential meal that can be
eaten with a variety of other dishes. So follow along and make sure you try it too.

Ingredients

1 cup of rice
1 table spoon salt
cooking oil
2 cups of water

instructions

  1. boil 2 cups of water
  2. add salt and cooking oil
  3. add 1 cup of rice
  4. let it cook until it is dry

Serve when hot with stew of your choice! Enjoy Leave a comment if you tried it and also checkout other recipes below.
